<p><strong>The Cloud Height Ceilometer Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Laser-drum Ceilometer</li><li>Optical Ceilometer</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The cloud height ceilometer market comprises two main types: laser-drum ceilometers and optical ceilometers. Laser-drum ceilometers utilize laser technology to accurately measure cloud height by emitting laser pulses and analyzing the reflected signals. They provide precise, real-time data, making them ideal for aviation and meteorological applications. On the other hand, optical ceilometers employ light-based methods, typically using LED or infrared light, to detect cloud presence and height. Both types serve critical roles in weather monitoring and atmospheric research.</p></p>
