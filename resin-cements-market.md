<p><ul><li>Light-Cured Resin Cements</li><li>Dual-Cured Resin Cements</li><li>Auto-Cured Resin Cements</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The resin cements market comprises three main types: light-cured, dual-cured, and auto-cured resin cements. Light-cured resin cements require exposure to a specific light source to harden, making them ideal for precise application. Dual-cured resin cements combine both light and chemical curing, providing versatility in challenging environments. Auto-cured resin cements set through a chemical reaction without the need for light, ensuring reliable adhesion in various situations. These options cater to diverse dental restoration needs, enhancing clinical outcomes.</p></p>
