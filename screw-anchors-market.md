<p><p>Screw anchors are essential fastening devices used for securing structures to the ground. The market primarily includes stud anchors and sleeved anchors. Stud anchors feature a threaded metal rod that enables deep penetration into the substrate, providing robust holding power. Conversely, sleeved anchors consist of a sleeve that allows for expansion upon installation, creating a secure grip in various materials. Both types cater to construction and engineering needs, offering reliability in applications such as building foundations and heavy equipment support.</p></p>
