<p><p>The high-purity zeolite market encompasses two main types: high-purity natural zeolite and high-purity synthetic zeolite. Conversely, high-purity synthetic zeolite is engineered through chemical processes, offering tailored properties and consistent quality, ideal for applications in catalysis and the petrochemical industry. Both types serve diverse sectors, driven by their high absorbent and molecular sieving capabilities.</p></p>
