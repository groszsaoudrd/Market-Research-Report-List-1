<p><strong>The Disposable Blood Dialyzer Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>High-Flux Dialyzer</li><li>Low-Flux Dialyzer</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The disposable blood dialyzer market is categorized into high-flux and low-flux dialyzers, each serving distinct patient needs in hemodialysis. High-flux dialyzers feature larger pore sizes, allowing for the efficient removal of larger molecules and toxins, making them suitable for patients with higher levels of uremic toxins. In contrast, low-flux dialyzers possess smaller pores, primarily removing smaller waste products. They are often used for patients with less complex needs, focusing on adequate solute clearance without extensive ultrafiltration.</p></p>
