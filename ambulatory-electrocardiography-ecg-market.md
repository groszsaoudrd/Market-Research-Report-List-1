Stress ECG Devices, designed to monitor heart performance during physical stress, commonly used in exercise testing. </p><p>3. Holter Monitors, portable devices that provide long-term cardiac monitoring over 24 to 48 hours, aiding in the detection of intermittent abnormalities.</p></p>
