<p><p>Phase Change Thermal Interface Materials (PCTIM) are innovative substances designed to enhance heat transfer between surfaces, particularly in electronic devices. The market for PCTIM comprises two main types: thermal pads and thermal pastes. Thermal pads are pre-formed sheets that provide consistent thickness and ease of application, ensuring effective thermal conductivity. In contrast, thermal pastes are viscous fluids that fill microscopic gaps between surfaces, offering superior thermal performance by conforming to irregularities. Both types play crucial roles in thermal management solutions.</p></p>
