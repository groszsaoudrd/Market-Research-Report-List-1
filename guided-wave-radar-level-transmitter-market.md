<p><p>Guided wave radar level transmitters utilize electromagnetic waves to measure the level of materials in various applications. Rod level transmitters are typically used for shorter measurement ranges, providing precise readings in applications like tanks and silos. They consist of a single probe that extends into the material. Cable level transmitters, on the other hand, employ a cable as the sensing element, enabling measurements over longer distances and complex geometries, making them ideal for taller vessels and challenging environments.</p></p>
