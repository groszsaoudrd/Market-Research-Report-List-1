<p><p>The photovoltaic power generation system market consists of three main types. Stand-alone solar photovoltaic systems operate independently, providing power in remote areas without grid access. Grid-connected solar photovoltaic systems are linked to the electrical grid, allowing for energy exchange and increased reliability. Distributed solar photovoltaic systems generate power close to where it is consumed, typically on residential or commercial rooftops, enhancing energy efficiency and reducing transmission losses. Each type addresses specific energy needs and enhances sustainability in power generation.</p></p>
