<p><p>MS Polymer Hybrid Adhesives and Sealants are classified into two main market types: one-component and two-component systems. One-component products are ready-to-use, requiring no mixing and curing upon exposure to moisture, making them convenient for quick applications. In contrast, two-component systems involve a mixing process before application, typically offering enhanced performance and tailored properties, ideal for demanding environments. Both types serve various industries, including construction and automotive, providing flexible bonding and sealing solutions with superior durability and adhesion.</p></p>
