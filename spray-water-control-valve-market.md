<p><p>The Spray Water Control Valve market primarily consists of two main types: 2-Way Type and 3-Way Type valves. The 2-Way Type valve functions to either start or stop the water flow, acting as a simple on/off mechanism, ideal for straightforward applications. In contrast, the 3-Way Type valve allows for both flow direction control and mixing capabilities, making it versatile for complex systems like cooling and heating. Both types are essential in agricultural, industrial, and HVAC applications for precise water management.</p></p>
