<p><ul><li>Slow Juicers</li><li>Quick Juicers</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The home fruit and vegetable juicer market is primarily divided into slow juicers and quick juicers. Slow juicers, or masticating juicers, operate at lower speeds, effectively extracting juice from fruits and vegetables while preserving nutrients and minimizing oxidation. They are ideal for leafy greens and harder produce. Quick juicers, or centrifugal juicers, employ high-speed blades to rapidly process fruits and vegetables, making them suitable for those seeking convenience and speed. However, they may compromise on nutrient retention and juice quality.</p></p>
