<p><ul><li>Powder Coatings</li><li>Water-borne Coatings</li><li>Solvent-borne Coatings</li><li>Radiation Curable Coatings</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The OEM coatings market comprises several key types tailored for different applications. Powder coatings are dry powders that provide a durable finish through electrostatic application and curing. Water-borne coatings utilize water as a solvent, offering lower volatile organic compound emissions. Solvent-borne coatings use organic solvents for application, delivering a smooth finish but potentially higher environmental impact. Radiation curable coatings cure quickly using UV or electron beam technology, providing high durability and efficiency, making them suitable for various industrial uses.</p></p>
