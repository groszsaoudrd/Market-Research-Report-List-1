<p><strong>The Wafer Inspection Device Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Optical Wafer Inspection</li><li>E-Beam Wafer Inspection</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The wafer inspection device market can be categorized into two main types: optical wafer inspection and e-beam wafer inspection. Optical wafer inspection utilizes light to detect defects on semiconductor wafers, offering high throughput and resolution for surface analysis. In contrast, e-beam wafer inspection employs electron beams for detailed inspection, providing higher precision by identifying submicron defects but generally at slower speeds. Each method serves specific needs in semiconductor manufacturing, emphasizing quality control and defect management in the production process.</p></p>
