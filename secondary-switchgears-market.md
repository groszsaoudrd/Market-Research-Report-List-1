<p><strong>The Secondary Switchgears Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Gas-insulated</li><li>Vacuum-insulated</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The secondary switchgears market is primarily categorized into gas-insulated and vacuum-insulated types. Gas-insulated switchgear (GIS) uses gas, typically SF6, for insulation and operates in compact spaces, making it ideal for urban settings. In contrast, vacuum-insulated switchgear (VIS) utilizes a vacuum as the insulating medium, ensuring high reliability and minimal maintenance. Both types are essential in modern power distribution systems, providing efficient operation, safety, and reducing the footprint of electrical infrastructure in various applications.</p></p>
