<p><p>The moisture cure adhesive market is divided into two main types: rigid moisture cure adhesives and soft moisture cure adhesives. Rigid moisture cure adhesives are designed for applications requiring high strength and structural integrity, often used in construction and industrial settings. In contrast, soft moisture cure adhesives offer flexibility, making them ideal for applications that require movement or vibration absorption, such as in automotive and consumer goods. Both types utilize moisture for curing, providing durability and enhanced performance in their respective uses.</p></p>
