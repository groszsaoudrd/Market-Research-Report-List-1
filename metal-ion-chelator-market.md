<p><p>The metal ion chelator market is categorized into types based on their mechanisms and applications. Precipitation agents facilitate the removal of metal ions by forming insoluble compounds. Complexing agents bind metal ions to prevent reactivity, often used in water treatment and agriculture. Phosphates serve to stabilize metal ions, reducing toxicity and enhancing solubility. Organic polyphosphoric acids act as multifunctional chelators, improving nutrient availability and metal ion mobility in soil, thus playing a crucial role in environmental management and industrial processes.</p></p>
