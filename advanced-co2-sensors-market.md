<p><p>The advanced CO2 sensors market primarily features two types: NDIR (Non-Dispersive Infrared) sensors and chemical CO2 sensors. NDIR sensors use infrared light absorption to measure CO2 concentration, offering high accuracy and reliability, making them ideal for indoor air quality monitoring and industrial applications. In contrast, chemical CO2 sensors utilize specific chemical reactions to detect CO2 levels, often being simpler and cost-effective. Both types play crucial roles in environmental monitoring and ensuring optimal conditions in various settings.</p></p>
