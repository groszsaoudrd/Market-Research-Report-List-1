<p><strong>The Waterproof Fabrics Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Breathable Waterproof Fabrics</li><li>Non-Breathable Waterproof Fabrics</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The waterproof fabrics market is primarily divided into two types: breathable and non-breathable waterproof fabrics. Breathable waterproof fabrics allow moisture vapor to escape while preventing water penetration, making them ideal for outdoor activities and sportswear; they enhance comfort by regulating temperature and moisture. In contrast, non-breathable waterproof fabrics completely block water but retain moisture, making them suitable for applications where exposure to water is more critical than comfort, such as protective clothing and industrial uses.</p></p>
