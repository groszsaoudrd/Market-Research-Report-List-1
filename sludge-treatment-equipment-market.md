<p><p>The sludge treatment equipment market comprises several types, including screw presses, centrifuges, belt filter presses, and others. Screw presses efficiently dewater sludge by applying mechanical pressure, while centrifuges utilize rotational force to separate solids from liquids. Belt filter presses use a continuous belt system to filter out moisture from sludge. Other equipment may include diaphragm presses and decanters, which serve similar dewatering and separation functions, catering to various industrial and municipal applications in wastewater treatment and sludge management.</p></p>
