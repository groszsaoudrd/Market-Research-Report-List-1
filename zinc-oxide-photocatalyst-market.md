<p><p>The Zinc Oxide Photocatalyst market comprises three main types: photocatalyst solutions, photocatalyst particles, and photocatalyst powders. Photocatalyst solutions are liquid formulations containing zinc oxide that can be applied to surfaces for enhanced photocatalytic reactions. Photocatalyst particles are fine solid forms of zinc oxide that facilitate various chemical processes under light exposure, often used in coatings and air purification systems. Photocatalyst powders are dry, powdered forms that can be utilized in industrial applications and environmental remediation, promoting degradation of pollutants.</p></p>
