<p><strong>The Firestop Putty Sticks Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Intumescent</li><li>Cementitious</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Firestop putty sticks are critical in preventing the spread of fire and smoke through openings in fire-rated barriers. The market for these products is primarily divided into two types: intumescent and cementitious. Intumescent putty sticks expand when exposed to high temperatures, forming a char that seals gaps, while cementitious putty sticks provide a robust, permanent firestop solution that hardens over time. Both types serve vital roles in ensuring structural integrity and safety in fire protection systems.</p></p>
