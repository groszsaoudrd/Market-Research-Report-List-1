<p><ul><li>Flat Absorbing Materials</li><li>Pyramidal Absorbing Materials</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Millimeter wave absorbing materials are categorized primarily into flat absorbing materials and pyramidal absorbing materials. Flat absorbing materials feature a uniform surface, designed to efficiently absorb electromagnetic waves across a broad spectrum within millimeter wave frequencies. They are commonly utilized in advanced communication systems and radar applications. Pyramidal absorbing materials, on the other hand, have a structured pyramid shape that enhances wave absorption through multiple reflection and refraction angles. This design is particularly effective for mitigating radar cross-section in various defense and aerospace applications.</p></p>
