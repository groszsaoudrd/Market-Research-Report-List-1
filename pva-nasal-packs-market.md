<p><p>The PVA nasal packs market can be categorized into two main types: with airway tube and without airway tube. PVA nasal packs with airway tubes are designed for easier ventilation and drainage, facilitating airflow while managing nasal bleeding or post-surgical recovery. Conversely, PVA nasal packs without airway tubes provide effective nasal packing and support healing but do not include a dedicated ventilation feature. Both types are essential in clinical settings for controlling nasal hemorrhage and aiding recovery from nasal procedures.</p></p>
