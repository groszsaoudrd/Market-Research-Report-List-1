<p><p>The waterproof magnetic reed switch market is categorized into three main types: Form A, Form B, and Form C. Form A switches are normally open, activating when a magnetic field is present. Form B switches are normally closed, deactivating in response to a magnetic field. Form C switches combine both functions, featuring one normally open and one normally closed circuit. Each type has unique applications in security systems, industrial automation, and consumer electronics, tailored to specific operational needs and environments.</p></p>
