<p><p>The Aluminum Beverage Package market is primarily segmented into two types: three-piece and two-piece cans. Three-piece cans consist of a body, top, and bottom made from separate aluminum sheets, providing flexibility in sizes and shapes. Conversely, two-piece cans are formed from a single aluminum sheet, which is drawn and shaped into a seamless body with a separate lid. Two-piece cans are typically lighter and more efficient for manufacturing, leading to increased adoption in various beverage industries.</p></p>
