<p><p>The Light Box market includes three main types of lighting technologies: LED, EEFL, and T4 fluorescent bulbs. LED light boxes are energy-efficient, long-lasting, and provide bright illumination, making them popular for various applications. EEFL, or Extended Electronic Fluorescent Lamps, offer improved efficiency and longer life than traditional fluorescent options, suitable for larger displays. T4 fluorescent bulbs, characterized by their compact size and high brightness, are often used in slim light boxes and accent lighting. Each type offers distinct advantages for different settings.</p></p>
