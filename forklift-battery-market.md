<p><p>The forklift battery market comprises mainly two types: lead-acid batteries and lithium-ion (Li-ion) batteries. Lead-acid batteries are traditional, cost-effective, and widely used, offering reliable performance for moderate usage. However, they require regular maintenance and have a shorter lifespan. In contrast, Li-ion batteries are gaining popularity due to their higher energy density, longer life, and lower maintenance needs. They enable faster charging and offer better efficiency, making them a preferred choice for modern, high-demand warehouse operations.</p></p>
