<p><ul><li>Re-circulating Air Curtains</li><li>Non-re-circulating Air Curtains</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Air curtains are devices designed to create an invisible barrier of air, separating two environments while allowing easy passage. The market is primarily divided into two types: re-circulating air curtains, which recirculate indoor air to maintain energy efficiency and reduce temperature loss, and non-re-circulating air curtains that draw air from outside. Re-circulating models are typically used in climate-controlled spaces, whereas non-re-circulating types are suited for high-traffic doors where outdoor air is less of a concern.</p></p>
