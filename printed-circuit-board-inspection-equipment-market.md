<p><strong>The Printed Circuit Board Inspection Equipment Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Automatic Optical Inspection (AOI)</li><li>Automatic X-ray Inspection (AXI)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Printed Circuit Board (PCB) inspection equipment market includes Automatic Optical Inspection (AOI) and Automatic X-ray Inspection (AXI) systems. AOI utilizes high-resolution cameras to detect visual defects on PCBs, such as misaligned components or solder joint issues, ensuring immediate feedback during assembly. AXI, on the other hand, employs X-ray technology to inspect hidden solder joints and internal layers of multilayer boards, providing a deeper analysis of component integrity and reliability. Both methods enhance quality assurance in electronic manufacturing.</p></p>
